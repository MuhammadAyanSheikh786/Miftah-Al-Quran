About Us – Miftah al Quran

Miftah al Quran is an online Qur'an learning platform created especially for Muslims living abroad. We provide one-to-one live sessions with certified Qur’an teachers, offering Tajweed, Qaida, Recitation, Hifz, and Islamic studies for all ages worldwide.

Our goal is to make Qur’an learning easy, flexible, and authentic for every student regardless of location.
